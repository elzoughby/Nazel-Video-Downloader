Features

Simple User Interface

Nazel has the clean user interface that you can quickly get familiar with. It uses the evident colored icons to express its options. The new download options are categoized and distributed in a colored groups to provide easy and usable window with quick options picking.


Playlist download

With Nazel you can download all the playlist items or select a specific items from it. You can download playlists from youtube and dailymotion, albums and groups from vimeo, courses from udemy, lynda and pluralsight, sets, playlists and stations from soundcloud and more collections from other video and audio streaming websites.

Clipboard monitor

Whenever you copy a video URL, Nazel will detect that and show a notification in the bottom-right corner of the screen to ask you If you want to download that video. The notification message appears for 5 seconds. when you click on it, the new download window shows up.



Download Queue

You can start new downloads immidiately or you can add them to the Queue for later downloading. The Queue can be started easily from the system tray icon or by starting one of its items. Whenever you start it, Nazel will downlaod its items one by one consecutively.
